SUPPORT TO PREVENT IDENTITY FRAUD

Use our real-time ID verification and face matching solutions to ensure that users are who they say they are.

Customers only have to do two things: a) use desktop, tablet or smartphone cameras to scan their ID’s and b) provide a selfie.

Genuine-ID verifies the authenticity of the ID based on a multitude of fully automated security checks.

Our face matching algorithm is astoundingly accurate. It quickly verifies the customer’s identity based on the photograph and the ID provided.

ENSURE AML/KYC COMPLIANCE FOR IDENTITY VERIFICATION

Genuine-ID performs reliable document verification and accurate face matching to meet Know Your Customers (KYC) requirements, even on mobile devices.
